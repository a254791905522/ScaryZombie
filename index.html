🏚️ About the Game

Welcome to Scary Zombie Teacher Neighbor, a terrifying horror adventure game packed with suspense, puzzles, and stealth gameplay. Your zombie teacher has turned into a nightmarish neighbor and trapped you inside a creepy house. Your only mission: escape without getting caught. Sneak through dark corridors, hide behind furniture, solve cryptic puzzles, and plan your escape carefully — one wrong move and the zombie teacher will find you.

🎮 How to Survive

  • Avoid the terrifying zombie teacher lurking around every corner
  • Explore a creepy house full of dark secrets and hidden rooms
  • Solve challenging puzzles to unlock doors and discover escape routes
  • Hide, sneak, and stay completely silent to survive
  • Collect crucial items and plan your escape with precision
  • Navigate 3D maps designed to keep you on edge
